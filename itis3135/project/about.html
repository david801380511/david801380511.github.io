Our Story

Starting in a small kitchen in Charlotte, our passion for authentic sourdough began in 2020. What started as a hobby during challenging times grew into a beloved local bakery, serving our community with naturally leavened breads made from organic ingredients.

Our Process

The Starter

Our sourdough starter, lovingly maintained daily, is the heart of every loaf we bake.

Slow Fermentation

Each dough ferments for 24-36 hours, developing complex flavors and improved digestibility.

Hand Shaping

Every loaf is carefully shaped by hand, preserving the dough's natural structure.

Wood-Fired Baking

Baked in our traditional wood-fired oven for the perfect crust and crumb.

Our Values

Quality Ingredients

We use organic, locally-sourced flour and maintain relationships with regional farmers to ensure the finest ingredients in every loaf.

Traditional Methods

No shortcuts - just time, patience, and respect for traditional baking methods passed down through generations.

Community Focus

We believe in building community through good bread and meaningful connections with our neighbors.
