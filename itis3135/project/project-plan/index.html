Page Design Details

Home Page

  • Purpose: Welcome visitors and highlight the bread
  • Audience: General visitors, bread enthusiasts
  • Content: Hero image, short intro, call-to-action ("Try It")
  • UI Elements: Navigation menu, CTA button
  • Actions: Navigate to other pages, learn about the bread

About Page

  • Purpose: Share background on the bread and the baker
  • Audience: Visitors interested in the story or process
  • Content: Short bio, baking process, philosophy
  • UI Elements: Text sections, optional photo(s)
  • Actions: Read content

Menu Page

  • Purpose: Show available sourdough varieties
  • Audience: Potential customers and curious visitors
  • Content: List of sourdough types with descriptions
  • UI Elements: Filter buttons, list or card layout
  • Actions: Browse bread types

Gallery Page

  • Purpose: Show visual quality of the bread
  • Audience: Anyone interested in seeing the final product
  • Content: High-quality images of bread, baking process
  • UI Elements: Image grid, lightbox for larger view
  • Actions: Click to enlarge, scroll through photos

Contact Page

  • Purpose: Allow people to reach out with questions or interest
  • Audience: People interested in trying the bread or learning more
  • Content: Contact form, email address, optional location info
  • UI Elements: Form fields, submit button
  • Actions: Submit a message
  • Validation: Required fields, basic email format check
